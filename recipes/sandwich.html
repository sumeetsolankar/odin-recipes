Sandwich

Description

A grilled sandwich is being freshly prepared,with crispy toasted bread and a flavorful vegetable filling visible inside.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Cheese
  • Mixed vegetables(like onions,tomatoes,capsicum)
  • Green chutney or herb spread
  • fresh basil or spinach leaves

Steps

  1. Prepare filling

    2. Mix chopped vegetables(onion,tomato,capsicum).

  2. Add cheese

    3. Grate or place cheese into mixture for a creamy taste.

  3. Prepare bread

    4. Tke 2 slices of bread and spread butter and green chutney

  4. Fill the sandwich

    5. Add the vegetable-cheese mixture between the bread slices

  5. Grill it

    6. place the sandwich in pan or sandwich

  6. Cut & serve

    7. Cut into pieces and serve hot

Pizza

Description

A freshly baked pizza is sliced and served,topped with melted cheese and colorful vegetables.
surronded by sauces and fresh ingredients,it looks hot,flavorful and ready to eat.

Ingredients

  • Pizza base
  • Cheese(melted)
  • Capsicum(green bell pepper)
  • onion
  • Tomato
  • corn
  • Herbs & chili flakes
  • pizza sauce

Steps

  1. Prepare dough/base

    2. Use a ready pizza base or prepare dough and roll it out.

  2. Add sauce

    3. Spread pizza sauce evenly on the base.

  3. Add toppings

    4. Place chopped capsicum ,onion,tomoto and corn on top.

  4. Add cheese

    5. Sprinkle generous grated cheese over the toppings.

  5. Season it

    6. Add chili flakes and herbs for extra flavor

  6. Bake pizza

    7. Bake in a preheated over at 200oc for 10-15 minutes until cheese melts and crust is golden.

  7. Slice & serve

    8. Cut into slice and serve hot with sauces.

Burger

Description

A juicy layered burger is freshly assembed with melted cheese,lettuce and patties stacked bettween soft bun

Ingredients

  • Burger buns
  • Two patties(veg or meat)
  • Cheese slices
  • Lettuce
  • onion slices
  • Tomato
  • Mushrooms
  • Fresh herbs(like thyme)

Steps

  1. Prepare patties

    2. Shape veg or meat mixture into patties and season with alt and pepper.

  2. Cook patties

    3. cook on a pan until browned and fully done on both sides.

  3. Toast buns

    4. Lightly toast burger buns with a lillte butter.

  4. Melt cheese

    5. Place cheese on the pattie while hot o it melts.

  5. Assemble burger

    6. Layer bun,lettuce,patty,onion,tomato,mushrooms and another patty if desired.

  6. Add sauce & serve

    7. Spread mayo or ketchup,cover with top bun and serve hot.
